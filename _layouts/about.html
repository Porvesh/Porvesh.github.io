About Me

Hello! I'm Porvesh Balasubramanian, a Computer Science student at the University of Michigan. My involvement extends beyond the classroom to the Taekwondo club and the Michigan Cricket Club. I have a strong interest in Systems, Algorithms, and Robotics, and I am driven by the excitement of solving complex problems and bringing innovative concepts into reality.

Beyond academics, I'm passionate about photography, music, and sports, with a particular fondness for Cricket and Tennis. Rafael Nadal and Sachin Tendulkar are sports heroes of mine. I play Division 1 cricket in the Michigan Cricket League and enjoy spending watching games.

Currently, I am involved in the Real-Time Computing Lab as a Robotics Research Assistant. Last summer, I had the wonderful experience of working as a Networking Engineer at Northrop Grumman. I'm always ready for a chat about tech, sports, or any engaging topic.

If you're interested in exchanging ideas or just connecting, feel free to reach out on LinkedIn or via Email.
